# WORLD BIBLE: Riverstone

## Overview
Riverstone is a quiet English town where two societies live in parallel: humans (who follow schedules and own pets) and animals (who have their own secret criminal underworld, the Meow-fia).

## Locations

### Sentinel Ridge
- Hilltop on the edge of Riverstone
- Houses the town's main transmitter station (radio, TV, emergency signals)
- Steep climb, winding paths, old stone markers
- Simba plans to use the transmitter to broadcast a "liberation signal" that will trigger all animals in town to simultaneously raid food storage locations
- Climax location — final showdown at the transmitter

### The Glass Vaults (from Book 2)
- Shopping center with high-security deli vault
- Maya's parents run security here
- Simba's Book 2 heist location
- Now has upgraded security after Book 2 breach

### Highspire Ruins (from Book 1)
- Ancient ruins outside town
- Site of the annual picnic
- Where the Salami Saboteur mystery began
- Abandoned stone structures, good for secret meetings

### Willow Creek Academy
- Bea's school
- Year 4 classroom
- Science Fair location (Book 2)
- Mr. Hendricks is the head teacher

### The Crumb & Kettle
- Local café on the high street
- Run by Mrs. Patel (possible human collaborator with Simba)
- Serves the best pastries in Riverstone
- Barnaby considers their sausage rolls "acceptable"

### The Thames River
- Runs through Riverstone
- The Mud Flats (Book 1 crossing point)
- Simba's boats are moored here
- Simba's Boathouse HQ (from Book 1, now abandoned)

### Riverstone Museum
- Where the Sovereign Stilton was nearly stolen (Book 1)
- Now has upgraded security
- Houses historical exhibits about Riverstone's founding

## Technology Level
- Modern day (smartphones, tablets, standard gadgets)
- Animals use surprisingly sophisticated toy-based tech (submersibles, walkie-talkies, sensor jammers)
- Maya's DIY gadgets are increasingly advanced
- The Unbreakable Deli Vault (Book 2) uses biometric locks

## Magic System (Pseudo-Scientific Telepathy)
- Barnaby can project thoughts exclusively to Bea
- Cause: shared electrical shock from an old radio in Sentinel Ridge
- Evolved in Book 2: Bea can now "ping" other animals (causes headaches)
- Secret bond — no other human can hear animals

## Animal Society Rules
- Animals have their own hierarchy and economy
- Currency: high-value snacks (salami, artisan cheese, roast meat)
- Informants: pigeons (the Aviary Network), frogs
- The Meow-fia: Simba's criminal syndicate
- The Canine Resistance: Dogs who reject both the Meow-fia and the leash
- The Squirrel Saboteurs: Guerrilla-style park territory warriors
