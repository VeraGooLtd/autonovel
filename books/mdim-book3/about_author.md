## **About the author**

Daniel Ryan writes science fiction, informed by a lifelong fascination with space, science, and emerging technology. His career spans over 27 years in Data Management, currently leading Customer Education for a software firm. Daniel uses this experience to build detailed, logical sci-fi worlds. He is passionate about crafting immersive adventures and enjoys the careful process of world-building and character creation. Daniel lives with his family and finds inspiration in sharing the wonders of science and imagination through his stories.
