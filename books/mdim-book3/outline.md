# OUTLINE: The Sentinel Ridge Showdown (Book 3)

## Act 1: The Frame-Up (Chapters 1-8)

### Chapter 1: The Morning After
- Bea wakes up with a headache (telepathic ping side effect)
- Barnaby is grumpier than usual — someone has been sleeping on his rug
- Mummy announces a "Neon Violet" day (the most chaotic schedule color)
- Doodle: [Barnaby's rug with mysterious fur on it]

### Chapter 2: The Accusation
- Mrs. Whiskers (a Persian cat) accuses Barnaby of stealing her diamond collar
- Evidence: Barnaby's fur found at the scene
- Barnaby is indignant — "I have STANDARDS. I don't steal jewelry."
- Bea knows he's being framed but can't prove it
- Doodle: [Barnaby in a police lineup with other dogs]

### Chapter 3: The Meow-fia Strikes
- Multiple pets report thefts — all evidence points to Barnaby
- Simba's signature: a tiny paw print in blue-gray mud
- The animal underworld turns against Barnaby
- Bea's detective notebook: she starts tracking the pattern
- Doodle: [Map of Riverstone with theft locations marked]

### Chapter 4: The Human Contact
- Bea notices Mrs. Patel (Crumb & Kettle) acting suspiciously
- She's been feeding strays behind the café
- Barnaby detects "feline hubris" near the café
- Maya's new gadget: a "Meow-fia Signal Detector" (from Book 2 tech)
- Doodle: [Mrs. Patel with a suspicious-looking cat following her]

### Chapter 5: The Ping
- Bea's telepathic ping reveals something unexpected
- She "hears" Simba's plan: a city-wide blackout and synchronized heist
- The target: ALL of Riverstone's food reserves
- The timing: during the upcoming Riverstone Festival
- Doodle: [Bea's head with radio waves coming out]

### Chapter 6: The Warning
- Bea tries to warn the adults — no one believes her
- Mummy thinks it's "imagination" from too many detective stories
- Maya believes the data but can't prove it
- Barnaby: "As usual, we are alone in our brilliance."
- Doodle: [Bea talking to adults who have thought bubbles full of question marks]

### Chapter 7: The Alliance
- Bea and Barnaby recruit their "Loyalists":
  - Henry the Horse (reluctant but brave)
  - Feathered Frank (terrified but loyal)
  - A few dogs from the Canine Resistance
- They plan to stop Simba at Sentinel Ridge
- Doodle: [Bea's "army" — a horse, a pigeon, a bulldog, and a 9-year-old]

### Chapter 8: The Climb Begins
- The team sets out for Sentinel Ridge
- Lulu "helps" by accidentally destroying Simba's lookout post
- They discover Simba has human collaborators
- Doodle: [The team climbing Sentinel Ridge with Lulu sliding back down]

## Act 2: The Infiltration (Chapters 9-18)

### Chapter 9: The Boathouse
- The team stops at Simba's old Boathouse HQ (from Book 1)
- They find Simba's plans: detailed maps of every food storage location in Riverstone
- Duke appears — confrontation with Barnaby
- Barnaby realizes Duke isn't evil, just following orders
- Doodle: [Barnaby and Duke facing off, both looking uncomfortable]

### Chapter 10: The Signal
- Maya intercepts a transmission: Simba's signal will broadcast at midnight
- The signal will trigger all animals to raid simultaneously
- They have 48 hours to stop it
- Doodle: [Maya's gadget with a big red countdown timer]

### Chapter 11: The Crumb & Kettle
- Bea investigates Mrs. Patel's café
- Discovers Mrs. Patel has been feeding Simba's operatives
- But she's being blackmailed — Simba has her cat hostage
- Bea realizes Simba is more sophisticated than they thought
- Doodle: [Mrs. Patel with a tearful cat in a tiny cage]

### Chapter 12: The Midpoint Reversal
- Simba's men capture Barnaby
- He's put on "trial" by the animal underworld
- Bea must choose: save Barnaby or stop the signal
- She chooses both — splits the team
- Doodle: [Barnaby in a tiny courtroom with a cat judge]

### Chapter 13: The Rescue
- Lulu accidentally causes chaos at Simba's camp
- Her "tactical sabotage" frees Barnaby
- They escape using Henry the Horse as a distraction
- Doodle: [Lulu riding Henry through a camp of terrified cats]

### Chapter 14: The Regroup
- The team regroups at the base of Sentinel Ridge
- Barnaby admits: "You are a capable collaborator, Bea."
- Bea realizes she needs to trust her instincts, not just her data
- Doodle: [Bea and Barnaby high-fiving/paw-shaking]

### Chapter 15: The Plan
- They devise a plan to stop the signal:
  - Maya jams the frequency from below
  - Bea and Barnaby climb to the transmitter
  - Henry and the Loyalists create a diversion
- Doodle: [Bea's notebook with a tactical diagram]

### Chapter 16: The Festival
- Riverstone Festival begins — the town is distracted
- Simba's operatives position themselves at food locations
- Mummy's "Neon Violet" schedule accidentally creates roadblocks
- Doodle: [Mummy's schedule with arrows pointing everywhere]

### Chapter 17: The Blackout
- Simba triggers the blackout — Riverstone goes dark
- Panic in the streets
- Bea and Barnaby begin the final climb
- Doodle: [Riverstone in darkness with glowing cat eyes everywhere]

### Chapter 18: The Transmitter
- Bea and Barnaby reach the transmitter station
- Simba is waiting with Duke and his elite guard
- Standoff — Simba gives a dramatic speech about animal liberation
- Doodle: [Simba on top of the transmitter like a tiny general]

## Act 3: The Showdown (Chapters 19-25)

### Chapter 19: The Debate
- Bea argues with Simba about control vs. community
- Simba: "I am liberating animals from boring kibble and schedules!"
- Bea: "You're building a cult, not a community."
- Barnaby: "Also, your salami is substandard."
- Doodle: [Bea and Simba arguing with speech bubbles]

### Chapter 20: The Signal
- Simba activates the liberation signal
- Animals across Riverstone begin to move
- But Maya's jammer interferes — the signal is garbled
- Animals get confused — some raid, some just wander
- Doodle: [Animals walking in confused circles]

### Chapter 21: The Loyalists
- Henry the Horse leads a charge up Sentinel Ridge
- Feathered Frank's pigeon network spreads the word: "Simba is a fraud!"
- The Canine Resistance turns against the Meow-fia
- Doodle: [A wave of animals climbing the ridge]

### Chapter 22: The Duel
- Barnaby confronts Duke one final time
- Duke admits he's tired of Simba's ego
- Duke switches sides — "I'm muscle, not a fanatic."
- Together, they take down Simba's elite guard
- Doodle: [Barnaby and Duke back-to-back fighting cats]

### Chapter 23: The Salami Solution
- Bea uses the 12kg Artisan Salami (saved from Book 1) as a distraction
- Cats can't resist it — they abandon their posts to fight over the salami
- Simba is furious: "You fight with MEAT?!"
- Doodle: [Cats swarming a giant salami while Bea runs past]

### Chapter 24: The Arrest
- The adults finally arrive (Mummy called the police)
- Simba is captured — but he escapes in the chaos
- The Meow-fia is temporarily dismantled
- Barnaby is cleared of all charges
- Doodle: [Simba in a tiny police car looking furious]

### Chapter 25: The New Normal
- Riverstone returns to normal
- Bea is recognized as the town's "silent guardian"
- Barnaby gets a new rug (a gift from Mrs. Patel)
- The team celebrates at the Crumb & Kettle
- Final line: "My dog is still judging me. But now, the whole town knows he's right."
- Doodle: [Bea, Barnaby, Maya, Lulu, Henry, and Frank at a café table with the caption: "THE END... FOR NOW"]
